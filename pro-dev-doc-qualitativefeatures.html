Qualitative Features:

receptive;

  • good: open to new ideas and trying out new concepts
  • not so good: can become unfamiliar causing a loss of interest/productivity


methodical:

  • good: creates structure.
  • not so good: last minute changes may become frustrating.


focused:

  • good: helps maintain proper momentum to achieve goals
  • not so good: can be a cause for tunnel vision
